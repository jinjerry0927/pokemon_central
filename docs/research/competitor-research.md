## Current Takeaways

### OP.GG Pokemon Champions

- Strength: Korean UX, high trust, fast lookup, strong brand.
- Risk for us: difficult to beat on traffic and polish.
- Opportunity: provide more explanatory beginner routes and deeper team-building workflows.

### PkmnChamps

- Strength: Pokemon Champions-specific tools such as Pokedex, sample builds, parties, calculator, matchup chart, and stats.
- Opportunity: improve Korean onboarding, editorial guides, and one-screen desktop workflows.

### Pokemon Showdown

- Strength: battle simulator and team-building standard.
- Opportunity: integrate learning-focused explanations instead of competing as a simulator.

### Smogon / PokemonDB / Bulbapedia

- Strength: long-standing search authority and deep data.
- Opportunity: focus on Champions-specific filtering, Korean summaries, and curated recommendations.

### Damage Calculators

- Strength: utility-driven repeat use.
- Opportunity: make calculations understandable for beginners and connect calculation results directly to build/team decisions.

## Recommended Competitive Strategy

Do not compete as a generic Pokemon encyclopedia. Start as a desktop-first Korean Pokemon Champions guide hub where users can:

1. Search a Pokemon.
2. Understand its role.
3. Copy or adapt a recommended build.
4. Add it to a team.
5. Check weaknesses and speed tiers.
6. Run damage calculations from the same context.

## Research Gaps

- Public traffic data for smaller Pokemon Champions-specific sites is limited.
- Target reference site may require separate access or manual screenshots if access is blocked.
- Official Pokemon Champions data availability may change before or after release.
